# Privacy Policy for "Campfire (Idle Game)"

Effective: May 8, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Android application "Campfire (Idle Game)" (hereinafter the "App") handles user information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

### 1.1 Locally Stored Data

The App stores game progress (fire level, accumulated AP, item counts, active guardian animals, last open timestamp, etc.) only on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to the developer's servers. Uninstalling the App removes this data from your device.

### 1.2 Information Collected via Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

- Android Advertising ID
- IP address
- General device information (OS version, language, region, etc.)
- Ad interaction history (impressions, clicks)

For details, see Google's policies:

- Google Privacy Policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
- How Google uses information: <https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>

You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads via **Settings → Google → Ads** on your Android device.

## 2. Sharing with Third Parties

Other than the AdMob processing described above, the App does not share user information with third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Provider |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | Banner ad delivery | Google LLC |
| Google Play Services | Core app features, ad SDK support | Google LLC |

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Security

Because the App does not directly collect personal information, the data exposure risk on the developer's side is limited. Locally stored data is protected by the standard security mechanisms of the Android OS.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time without prior notice. Updates take effect when posted on this page. Major changes will be announced in the app or on the store listing.
